Dungeon Dome

Indie Game in development

Role: Lead Artist, 3D Artist, UI Designer & AI Prompter
Description: Dungeon Dome is a thrilling RPG where you and your party venture deep into procedurally generated dungeons. Each character is unique and one-of-a-kind, with their own strengths and quirks, just like the adventures that await you in the dark and treacherous depths. I was in charge of the art direction and I created 3D art for the characters and the environments. I also designed the UI of the menus and created the prompts used in character generation.
